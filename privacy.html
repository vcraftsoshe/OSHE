Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

OSHE and HARM are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what your rights are.

What we collect

When you use OSHE or engage with HARM, we may collect:

  • Your name, email address, and contact details when you sign up or get in touch
  • Organisation name and details provided during onboarding
  • Usage data relating to how you interact with the OSHE platform
  • Site sign-in data including worker and visitor logs
  • Incident reports, hazard records, and other H&S data you enter into the platform

How we use your information

  • To provide and improve the OSHE software and HARM consulting services
  • To communicate with you about your account, your trial, or your engagement with us
  • To send you relevant updates or information about our services (you can opt out at any time)
  • To meet our legal obligations

Who we share it with

We do not sell your data. We may share information with trusted third-party service providers who help us operate the platform, such as hosting and payment providers. These providers are bound by their own privacy obligations.

Data storage and security

Your data is stored securely. We take reasonable steps to protect it from unauthorised access, loss, or misuse. OSHE is hosted on New Zealand and Australian infrastructure.

Your rights

Under the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020, you have the right to access the personal information we hold about you and request corrections. To do so, get in touch with us directly.

Cookies

The OSHE platform uses cookies to keep you logged in and to understand how the platform is being used. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not work as expected.
