Common Questions

Good to know before you start.

Do I need a credit card to start the trial?

No. Sign up and get full access for 7 days with no payment details required.

What happens after the trial?

After your 7-day trial, if you want to continue jump on an onboarding call with the team where they will help you get set up.

Are the prices in NZD?

Yes. All pricing is in New Zealand dollars and excludes GST.

What counts as a user?

A user is anyone with a login to the OSHE platform. Workers who sign in via QR code on site do not count as users.

Is HARM consulting included in my OSHE subscription?

Launch and Growth include customised OHSM policies and procedures, onboarding, quarterly check-ins, ongoing support and advice, and incident investigation remote support. Extra consulting such as site reviews and Tōtika advisory work is available at member rates.

How does Enterprise pricing work?

Enterprise is priced by negotiation based on your organisation's size and needs. Get in touch with Vanessa to talk through what would work for you.
